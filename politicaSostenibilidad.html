Sustainable Development Policy

At Formula E Operations Limited, we are acutely aware of our role in the global sports landscape, not just as entertainers but as vanguards of a sustainable future. Founded with a mission to accelerate sustainable human progress, our championship is a laboratory for innovation and a platform where the most advanced electric vehicle technologies are tested in the white heat of competition. Set in the heart of the world's most iconic cities, the ABB FIA Formula E World Championship delivers spectacles of electric racing through a compelling mix of performance, sustainability, and innovation.

Our journey has been more than just organising races; it's been about changing perceptions, pushing the boundaries of technology and demonstrating that racing and reason can coexist harmoniously without compromise. Since 2015, we have been working in line with the ISO 20121 standard to reinforce our commitment generating positive social impacts while minimising our environmental impacts and continually improving our practices, adhering to legal and other requirements, and setting benchmarks in sustainable event management.

Combining racing and reason by enacting our 'Sustainable Excellence' blueprint that hardwires sustainability into Formula E's corporate strategy. We intend, in the long-term, to make our car evolve into a more efficient model, testing the most advanced technological developments and sustainable energy sourcing to showcase technology that can be transferred from race to road.

Our commitment to creating positive impact within the communities of our Host Cities across the world, with a strong emphasis on social inclusion, diversity, equity & inclusion. Formula Equity is equally dedicated to nurturing a healthy environment within our own ecosystem, ensuring that everyone can thrive and encouraging active engagement with the strategy across employees, teams & partners.

Continuously advancing our Carbon Management and Sustainability Operations plan with the aim of minimising environmental impact and reducing our carbon footprint. We are working towards an emissions reduction plan validated by the Science Based Target Initiative (SBTi).

Creating Value Through Values for all our stakeholders by implementing a set of tailored sustainability programmes that build strong and long-lasting collaborations. These programmes cover both environmental and social initiatives, according to each stakeholder’s activity in the event value chain and set of priorities, from the development of sustainable materials to the promotion of children’s education and welfare.
