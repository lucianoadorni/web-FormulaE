SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT POLICY

At Formula E Operations Limited, we are acutely aware of our role in the global sports landscape, not just as entertainers but as vanguards of a sustainable future.

Our journey has been more than just organising races. It's been about changing perceptions, pushing the boundaries of technology and demonstrating that racing and reason can coexist harmoniously.

Formula E is underpinned by four core values: Impact, I(vanov), Bravery, and Humanity. These values catalyse our efforts to create a positive impact, embrace optimism, challenge the status quo, and prioritise the wellbeing of communities.

Excellence

Combining racing and reason by enacting our 'Sustainable Excellence' blueprint that hardwires sustainability into Formula E's corporate strategy.

Equity

Our commitment to creating positive impact within the communities of our host cities across the world, with a strong emphasis on social inclusion, diversity.

Environment

Continuously advancing our Carbon Management and Sustainability Operations plan with the aim of minimising environmental impact.

Ecosystem

Creating value through values for all our stakeholders by implementing a set of tailored sustainability programmes.
